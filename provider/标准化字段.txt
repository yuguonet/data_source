下面的所有数据在返回前都需要做标准化处理
K线
{time, open, high, low, close, volume}
time: Unix 时间戳
open/high/low/close: OHLC 四价
volume: 成交量

Ticker 行情（fetch_quote / fetch_quotes_batch 返回）
Standard quote fields (from UStock/TwelveData):
{last, change, changePercent, high, low, open, previousClose, name, symbol}

last: 最新价
change: 涨跌额
changePercent: 涨跌幅 (%)
previousClose: 昨收价
name/symbol: 名称和代码
time: Unix 时间戳
